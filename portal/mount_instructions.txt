Add these two lines to main.py after the existing app.mount("/media"...) line:

from fastapi.staticfiles import StaticFiles

app.mount("/portal", StaticFiles(directory="portal", html=True), name="portal")

Then copy the civicpulse_web folder to your civicpulse backend folder and rename it to "portal":

civicpulse/
  portal/
    index.html
    css/
      style.css
    js/
      api.js
    pages/
      login.html
      dashboard.html
